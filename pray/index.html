Compatibility

Pray is designed for supported iPhone, iPad, and Android devices. Feature availability can vary by platform, operating-system version, device capability, region, permissions, account status, connectivity, and app-store availability. Voice input requires microphone and speech-recognition permission. Notifications require permission. Some content works offline after it has been saved or downloaded; AI, community, account, and purchase features require network access. Users must be at least 18 years old.
